I am currently an Assistant PRofessor at North Carolina A & T State University with the department of Computer Science . I have obtained my doctorate from the department of computer science at Georgia State University. I work with Dr. Yingshu Li and Dr. Zhipeng Cai as a part of my doctoratal research. I have completed my Masters from IIIT Delhi , India under the guidance of Dr. Somitra Sanadhya . In my masters, I have specialized in security and privacy and worked on a thesis named "Cryptanalysis of digital watermarking using SVM". I have completed my B.S degree from Osmania University, Hyderabad, Andhra Pradesh in 2010.

My current research interests include Privacy aware computing, Internet Of Things, social networks, big data and data mining. I am currently working on preserving security and privacy in online social media. You can go to news at USA Today, Electronic Privacy Information Center to understand the need for such research.
