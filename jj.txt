I am currently a computer science doctorate student at <a href="http://cs.gsu.edu">Georgia State University</a>. I work with <a href="https://grid.cs.gsu.edu/yli/">Dr. Yingshu Li</a> and <a href="https://grid.cs.gsu.edu/zcai/">Dr. Cai</a> as a part of my doctorate degree.
I have completed my Masters degree from <a href="http://iiitd.ac.in/">IIIT Delhi </a>, New Delhi under the guidance of <a href="https://sites.google.com/view/somitra">Dr. Somitra Sanadhya </a>. In my masters, I have specialized in security and privacy and worked on a thesis named "Cryptanalysis of digital watermarking using SVM". 
I have completed my B.S degree from <a href="http://www.osmania.ac.in/">Osmania University</a>, Hyderabad, Andhra Pradesh in 2010. 


<br><br>
My current research interests include Privacy aware computing, social networks, big data and data mining.
I am currently working on preserving security and privacy in online social media. You can go to news at 
<a href="http://www.usatoday.com/story/news/nation/2014/03/08/data-online-behavior-research/5781447/">USA Today</a>
<a href="https://epic.org/privacy/socialnet/">Electronic Privacy Information Center</a> to understand the need for such research.
